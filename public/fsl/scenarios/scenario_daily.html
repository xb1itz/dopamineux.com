Scenarios:

    Daily routine scenarios

    These scenarios oriented to bring causal rewards to users who are looking for information to digest or interct with community.

    Legend

    Each scenario represents a particular business case. What steps users take to interact with the system to complete their goals.
    Some of the steps have additional indicators that are explained below:

    • A trigger

      Represents a point or event where users are being engaged into the scenario.

    • Investment

      A step where a user has to spend mental effort to proceed to further steps of the scenario. This is usually the most challenging moment in the scenario.

    • AFK timeout

      Away From Keyword moment that represents a temporary break in the scenario, where users are not actively engaged in a system.

    • Notification

      Represents an automatic notification that engages user outside of a system.

    • The reward

      A motivator that rewards user for completing whole (or a part of) scenario.

    #1 Team oriented news feed

    • 1. Entry point

      The scenario begins when a user opens the app/site casually w/o any external trigger.

    • 2. Instant team feed

      Once user opens the app they automatically see a tailored content feed with recent news from their team. Basically we showing users what is the most important for them.
      Note: It super very important to balance a content, that there won't be duplicated pieces and user won't see all the same content every time, they checks the feed.

      • Pieces suggested by recommendation engine
      • Content from followed people
      • Team news highlights (scraped)
      • Team events
      • Upcoming matches
      • Ended matches

    #2 League oriented news feed (global news)

    • 1. Entry point

      The scenario begins when a user opens the app/site casually w/o any external trigger.

    • 2. Switch

      By default users see their team oriented feed, but here they switch to global league feed.

    • 2. Global feed

      Here they see all the top news in the league, is same way as in Scenario #1

    #3 Match history

    • 1. Entry point

      User click to view teams match history:

      • From navigation
      • From match card in any of the feeds
      • From particular match screen
      • Follows direct link (organic or search engine)
      • Using search???

    • 2. User lands on team/league match list

      Here we provide a list of all past and future matches for selected team/league:

      • Past matches display a score
      • Live matches are highlighted
      • Future matches display time and venue of the match

    #4 Special events

    • 1. Browsing a feed

      Scenario starts when users are browsing a feed (team or global)

    • 2. Special event

      If special event happens, system shows highlighted card for such event to fraw more focus.
      The event can be any one of the transfers, management changes, player purchases and any of other team-inportant news.
      Each special event may contain a special action related to it, although it may be viable for MVP.

    #5 Reading a comments

    • 1. Browsing a feed

      Scenario starts when users are browsing a feed (team or global)

    • 2. Comments

      By default user can see top discussion comments of any content piece.
      If content piece has many comments, the less relevant comments are hidden by default.

    • 3. Click through

      User clicks to show all comments

    • 4. Full comment list

      All comments for a certain content piece are displayed chronologically.
      Each comment also can have single level response thread on its own.

    #6 Reading reactions to own content

    • 1. Notification

      Scenario starts when user receives notification that someone reacted on their comment.

    • 2. Click through

      User clicks to show the particular comment

    • 3. Full comment view

      Full comment with all the reactions and replies are shown in focused view.

    #7 Commenting

    • 1. Focus

      Scenario begins when user has his focus on certain piece of content.

    • 2. Click through

      User clicks to post a comment.
      Alternatively they can click on instant reaction button (e.g. like/dislike).

    • 3. Comment form

      System provides single field comment form

    • 4. Entering a comment

      User enters his comment into forms and submits it

    • 5. Comment saved

      New comment is showed highlighted in comment thread.

    #8 Posting a reply

    • 1. Focus on the comment

      Scenario begins when user read the particular comment and has emotional reaction to it.

    • 2. Click through

      User clicks to post a reply.

    • 3. Reply form

      System provides single field reply form (Baically same as comment, just with referrence to another user)

    • 4. Entering a reply

      User enters his reply into forms and submits it

    • 5. Reply saved

      New reply is showed highlighted in comment thread.

    #9 React or reply on own content

    • 1. Notification

      Scenario starts when user receives notification that someone replied on their post or comment.

    • 2. Click through

      User clicks to show the particular comment/reply

    • 3. Focused reply/comment

      System shows the particular comment/reply highlighted

    • 4. Reply

      Scenario continues the same as Scenario #8

    #10 Share on SFL

    • 1. Focus

      Scenario begins when user has his focus on certain piece of content.

    • 2. Click through

      User clicks to share a particular post.

    • 3. Share form

      System provides a sharing screen with single field comment form where user may enter a share message.

    • 4. Share

      User can click to share instantly or enter additional message.

    • 5. Content piece is shared

      A reward message is showed.

      5.1. Shared content virality

      A shared content automatically is included into the feeds of user's followers.

      5.2. Share notification

      If the content piece was create by user, they receive a notification that it was shared.

    #11 Share on socials

    • 1. Focus

      Scenario begins when user has his focus on certain piece of content.

    • 2. Click through

      User clicks to share a particular post via selected Social network

    • 3. Share modal

      System provides a selection of Social networks to share the particular content on, and also ability to copy a short URL to specific content item.

    • 4. Click through

      User click to share content piece on particular newtwork.

    • 5. Share window

      A share window appears where user can publish a particular content in their social feed.
